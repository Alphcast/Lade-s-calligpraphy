Our Story

Crafting Faith Into
Timeless Art

At Lade's Calligraphy, we believe every word carries divine energy — especially when written with intention, skill, and love. Born from a deep reverence for Islamic art and the beauty of Arabic script, we create pieces that don't merely decorate spaces, they elevate them.

Every stroke is deliberate. Every piece is a conversation between faith and artistry. Based in Egbeda, Lagos, we craft bespoke Arabic calligraphy for homes, offices, weddings, and meaningful gifting — across Nigeria and beyond.
